# CineGrade — Cinematic Video Color Grading for iPhone

Professional color grading designed by a working colorist with 13 IMDB credits. Transform your iPhone video into cinematic footage in seconds.

## Features

**30 Cinematic Looks** — Handcrafted film emulations and cinematic moods, not algorithm-generated filters. Kodak, Fuji, CineStill emulations, teal & orange, golden hour, neon noir, and more.

**Precise Color Controls** — 11 professional grading sliders: Exposure, Contrast, Highlights, Shadows, Temperature, Tint, Saturation, Vibrance, Sharpness, Vignette, and Fade.

**Film Grain & Halation** — Authentic analog film texture with 5 grain presets (Fine 35mm, Heavy 35mm, Super 16, Super 8, Digital Noise) and cinematic halation glow on highlights.

**Cinematic Letterbox Mattes** — 9 aspect ratios including 2.39:1 Scope, 2.20:1 70mm, 2.00:1 Univisium, and more. Solid, gradient, or custom color styles.

**Scene Detection** — AI-powered cut detection with per-clip grading. Apply different grades to each scene while keeping a consistent global look.

**Real-Time Histogram** — Live RGB histogram overlay to monitor color balance as you grade.

**Grading Presets** — Save and load your grading recipes across projects.

**Custom LUT Import** — Import your own .cube LUT files for a fully custom pipeline.

**Platform-Optimized Export** — One-tap export presets for Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Feed, X (Twitter), Facebook, 4K, and Max Quality. Auto-saves to Camera Roll.

**Before/After Comparison** — Split-screen, side-by-side, and toggle comparison modes during live playback.

**100% On-Device** — All processing happens on your iPhone. No cloud uploads, no accounts, no tracking.

## FAQ

**How do I apply a cinematic look?**
Open a project, browse the Looks carousel at the bottom of the screen, and tap any look to apply it instantly. Use the intensity slider to blend between the original and graded image.

**How do I export my graded video?**
Switch to the Export tab, select a platform preset (YouTube, Instagram Reels, TikTok, etc.), and tap Export. The graded video is automatically saved to your Camera Roll.

**How do I import a custom LUT?**
In the Looks tab, tap the import button (Pro feature). Select a .cube file from your device. The imported LUT will appear in the Custom LUTs category.

**Can I use CineGrade with Apple Log footage?**
Yes. CineGrade includes a "Log to Cinema" look specifically designed for Apple Log footage shot on iPhone 15 Pro and later.

**Does CineGrade upload my videos anywhere?**
No. All video processing happens entirely on your iPhone. CineGrade does not require an internet connection, does not upload your content, and does not track your video data.

**How do I restore my Pro subscription on a new device?**
Open the app, go to the Info screen (tap the info icon on the project list), and tap "Restore Purchases" in the Pro section. Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID and will restore automatically.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to iPhone Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > CineGrade Pro > Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

**What video formats are supported?**
CineGrade supports all standard iPhone video formats including H.264, H.265/HEVC, ProRes, and Apple Log.

**What iPhone models are supported?**
CineGrade requires iPhone 11 or later running iOS 26 or newer.
